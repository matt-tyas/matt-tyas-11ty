About

Experienced design leader and company director at Manchester based media platform Finest Group.

I'm a polymath Designer highly experienced in product management, leadership, strategy, UX, accessibility, content design, user research and front-end engineering.

I work four days as a Principal Designer at the Co-op and one at my own business — Manchester’s Finest.

I believe in:

  • crafting outcome based visions and goals that align to customer and business needs and empower teams to solve problems
  • using researched based design to achieve user and business goals
  • prototyping in code and showing users early and often
  • the power of systems and efficiency to empower teams to innovate and move quickly
  • collaboration across disciplines to create the best outcomes
